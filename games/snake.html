About the Game

Snake is a classic arcade game that has been entertaining players since the late 1970s. In this digital adaptation, you control a snake that grows longer as it eats food. The challenge is to avoid colliding with the walls or your own tail as you navigate the playing field. This simple yet addictive gameplay has made Snake one of the most recognizable video games of all time.

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys or swipe gestures to control the snake's direction
  • Eat the food items that appear on the screen to grow longer
  • Avoid hitting the walls or your own tail
  • The game gets progressively harder as your snake grows longer
  • Try to achieve the highest score possible before the game ends

Features

  • Smooth, responsive controls for seamless gameplay
  • Progressive difficulty that increases with your score
  • Colorful, vibrant graphics with visual effects
  • Sound effects and background music to enhance the experience
  • Global leaderboard to compete with players worldwide
  • Statistics tracking for personal best scores and play time
