About the Game

Word Search is a classic puzzle game where you search for words hidden in a grid of letters. Words can be placed horizontally, vertically, diagonally, forwards, or backwards. Challenge your observation skills and vocabulary as you find all the hidden words!

How to Play

  • Find words from the list in the grid of letters
  • Click and drag to select letters that form a word
  • Words can be placed in any direction (horizontal, vertical, diagonal)
  • Words can also be spelled backwards
  • Find all words to complete the puzzle

Features

  • Multiple difficulty levels
  • Timer to track your speed
  • Score system with bonuses for fast finds
  • Hint system when you get stuck
  • Beautiful, responsive design
