About the Game

Custom Tic Tac Toe takes the classic game to the next level with enhanced features and customization options. This upgraded version maintains the simplicity that made the original game so popular while adding exciting new elements to keep gameplay fresh and engaging. Players can personalize their experience with various themes, board sizes, and gameplay modes.

How to Play

  • Choose from multiple board sizes (3×3, 4×4, or 5×5), or even upto 15×15)
  • Select your preferred mark style and game theme
  • Players take turns placing their mark in an empty square
  • The first player to get a row of marks (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins
  • Enjoy special power-ups and game modifiers for added strategy
  • Track your stats and climb the global leaderboard

Features

  • Multiple board sizes for varied gameplay complexity
  • Customizable themes, marks, and sound effects
  • Advanced AI opponents with adjustable difficulty levels
  • Special power-ups like "Swap," "Block," and "Extra Turn"
  • Online multiplayer with friends or random opponents
  • Comprehensive statistics tracking and achievements system
