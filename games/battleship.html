About the Game

Battleship is a classic naval combat strategy game where players take turns firing at each other's hidden fleet. The objective is to sink all of your opponent's ships before they sink yours. This digital adaptation brings the beloved board game to your screen with enhanced features and gameplay.

How to Play

  • Each player secretly places their fleet of ships on their grid
  • Players take turns calling out coordinates to fire at their opponent's grid
  • If a shot hits an enemy ship, it's marked as a hit; otherwise, it's a miss
  • Players must announce when a ship has been completely sunk
  • The first player to sink all of their opponent's ships wins

Features

  • Intuitive drag-and-drop ship placement
  • Real-time hit and miss animations with sound effects
  • Multiple difficulty levels when playing against AI
  • Online multiplayer with friends or random opponents
  • Customizable fleet configurations and game rules
  • Statistics tracking for wins, losses, and accuracy
