About the Game

Tic Tac Toe is a classic paper-and-pencil game that has been enjoyed by players of all ages for generations. This digital version brings the timeless game to your screen with enhanced features and gameplay. Players take turns marking X or O in a 3×3 grid, with the goal of placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row.

How to Play

  • The game is played on a 3×3 grid
  • Player 1 uses X, Player 2 uses O
  • Players take turns placing their mark in an empty square
  • The first player to get three marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins
  • If all squares are filled and no player has three in a row, the game ends in a draw

Features

  • Clean, intuitive interface with responsive design
  • Play against a friend or challenge our AI with multiple difficulty levels
  • Customizable game themes and mark styles
  • Game history tracking to review past matches
  • Achievement system to unlock rewards as you play
  • Statistics tracking for wins, losses, and draws
