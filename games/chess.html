About the Game

Chess is a classic strategy board game played between two players on an 8√ó8 grid with 64 squares. Each player begins with 16 pieces: one king, one queen, two rooks, two knights, two bishops, and eight pawns. The objective is to checkmate your opponent's king, where the king is under immediate attack and has no legal move to escape.

How to Play

  • White always moves first, followed by alternating turns with Black
  • Each piece has its own unique movement pattern
  • Capture opponent's pieces by moving your piece to their square
  • Special moves include castling, en passant, and pawn promotion
  • The game ends when a king is checkmated, a stalemate occurs, or players agree to a draw

Features

  • Beautiful, responsive chessboard with intuitive drag-and-drop interface
  • Move validation and highlighting of legal moves
  • Game timer with customizable time controls
  • Move history with algebraic notation
  • Piece promotion options when pawns reach the opposite rank
  • Board flipping to view from either player's perspective
