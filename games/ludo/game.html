Ludo Rules

  • Each player has 4 tokens that start in their home yard
  • Roll the dice to move your tokens around the board
  • You need to roll a 6 to move a token out of the home yard
  • Capture opponent tokens by landing on them, sending them back to their home yard
  • Safe zones (marked with stars) protect tokens from being captured
  • A player gets an extra turn when rolling a 6 or capturing an opponent's token
  • Navigate all four of your tokens to the center of the board to win
