About the Game

Fruit Slasher is an exciting arcade game where you slice fruits with swift swipes while avoiding bombs. Test your reflexes and precision as you cut through flying fruits to rack up points. The faster and more accurate your slices, the higher your score!

How to Play

  • Swipe across fruits to slice them in half
  • Slice multiple fruits with a single swipe for combo points
  • Avoid slicing bombs or it's game over
  • Try to slice all fruits before they fall off the screen
  • Beat your high score with each playthrough

Features

  • Colorful fruit emojis with satisfying slicing effects
  • Particle juice effects when fruits are sliced
  • Progressive difficulty as your score increases
  • High score tracking to challenge yourself
  • Smooth animations and responsive controls
